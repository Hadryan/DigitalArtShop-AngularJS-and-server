Introduction

Fan's Digital Art Shop is the dream of our CEO, Mr. Chi Ming Fan. It opens just now. It is a place for exhibition of the artworks that are made by our artists. It is also a purchase platform for the customers who love our artworks and want to buy them.

All the artworks are made from photos and special effects. Also, a descriptive title is given to each artwork. Every artwork is unique and it will only be sold to one customer.
